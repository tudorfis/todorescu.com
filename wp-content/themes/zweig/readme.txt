== ABOUT ZWEIG ==

Zweig is a minimalist Wordpress theme with a soft touch of orange, oriented towards a nice reading and writing experience on every possible device. It features a clean and minimalist one column design, a three widget area footer, a single-level menu header and full ligatures support. It's built on HTML5 and CSS3 and fully responsive.
